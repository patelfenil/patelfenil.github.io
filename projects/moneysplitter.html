Money Splitter

Splitting money made easy.

MOTIVATION

Weekend trips is something which every college student looks forward to, but with every weekend trip there comes the problem of splitting money. Existing systems like Splitwise required lengthy signup procedure, focused on one trip, provided unoptimized calculations and had no option for custom split,so I co-developed Money Splitter app.

FEATURES

  1. Multiple events

     Most of the apps found online focus on only one event, so if your trip includes more than one events like travelling,dinner,movies then you have to calculate separately for each event.But in money splitter all the events can be handeld at once.Moreover the results are optimized to make the cash transitions as minimum as possible.

  2. Custom participation and custom splits.

     The app has the functionality of excluding a person from a particular event in case of no participation.Moreover for a particular event it is not always necessary that only one person pays the whole bill thats why we have taken cases into consideration where more than one person contributes to pay the bill.
